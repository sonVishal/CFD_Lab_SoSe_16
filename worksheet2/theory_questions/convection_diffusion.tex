\section{Convective-/viscous term}


Consider the boltzmann equation, 
\begin{equation}
    \frac{\delta f}{\delta t} + \vec{v}\cdot\nabla{f} = -\Delta(f-f^{eq}),
\end{equation}

and the BGK approximation

\begin{equation}
    \Delta (f - f^{eq}) = -\frac{1}{\tau}(f - f^{eq})
\end{equation}
